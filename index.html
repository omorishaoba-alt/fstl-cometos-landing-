Executive Overview

FSTL operates in the Property Technology (PropTech) sector, focused on transforming land and landed property into structured, investable capital. COMET OS Phase I provides a scalable infrastructure to verify ownership, reduce fraud, and issue QR-secured receipts directly linked to FSTL First Bank transactions. Phase II will integrate Title Insurance, unlocking true capital access for verified assets.

  • Verification of land & landed property (residential, commercial, industrial)
  • Bank-confirmed payments with QR-secured receipts
  • Audit-ready digital ledger for regulators & investors
  • Structured asset transformation for capital enablement

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What does FSTL do?
A: FSTL transforms dead land and landed assets into verified, investable capital using COMET OS infrastructure.

Q: How does COMET OS work?
A: Identity verification, document validation, bank-confirmed payments, QR-secured receipts, and digital audit trails.

Q: Are payments safe?
A: Yes — all payments go to FSTL First Bank and are confirmed via QR receipts.

Q: What are trustee slots?
A: Limited participation opportunities for investors who fund FSTL’s infrastructure, giving oversight and early access to structured assets.

A: Phase I is verification only. Phase II will provide full title insurance.

A: 24–48 hours depending on property type and location.

Q: Why invest?
A: Verified assets become investor-ready, reducing risk and creating opportunities for structured capital participation.
